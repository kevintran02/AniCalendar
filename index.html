What is AniCalendar?

AniCalendar is a web application that helps anime enthusiasts keep track of upcoming anime releases and review completed series. With a user-friendly interface, you can easily find out when your favorite anime episodes are airing and share your thoughts on the ones you've watched.

Whether you're a casual viewer or a hardcore fan, AniCalendar is designed to enhance your anime-watching experience.

Join our community of anime lovers and never miss an episode again!
